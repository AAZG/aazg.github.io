What I do

Data Scientist | Data Analyst | Python Developer

I have experience in the field of data science, with a particular focus on extracting, cleaning, and analyzing large data sets from various sources, such as SQL, .csv, etc.

I have worked on several projects in the field, including one that used clustering algorithms such as KMeans, Agglomerative Clustering, DBSCAN, AffinityPropagation, MeanShift, and SpectralClustering to segment a company's customers, allowing them to tailor their products to the needs and specific customer behaviors.

Another project I worked on involved the analysis of digital fine needle aspiration (FNA) images of breast masses to determine the diagnosis of breast tissues in future tumor patients.

I have cleaned and preprocessed the data, performed univariate and multivariate analyses, scaled the data, and built a binary classification model using algorithms such as LogisticRegression, GaussianNaiveBayes, DecisionTreeClassifier, RandomForestClassifier, KNeighborsClassifier, SupportVectorMachine, BaggingClassifier, BoostingDecisionTree, and VotingClassifier.

In addition to these projects, I have also worked on text analysis projects using LDA and other techniques.

Throughout my work in data science, I have developed skills in data analysis, market segmentation, effective communication, and more.
